BimBamBop

Description

Bibimbap is one of the most well known Korean dishes. A rice bowl topped with all sorts of seasoned saut√©ed vegetables, marinated meat (usually beef), a fried egg sunny side up, finished with a sprinkle of sesame and generous dollop of a sweet-spicy-savoury Bibimbap sauce. It takes some time to gather the ingredients, but the end result is so worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of cooked rice
  • 2 ounces of thinly sliced tender beef (rib eye, sirloin, etc. or ground beef)
  • Assortment of fresh vegetables (beansprouts, spinach, cucumbers, zucchini, carrots, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp of gochujang sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp of sesame oil
  • Optional: 1 egg

Steps

  1. Place a serving of rice into a big bowl.
  2. Nicely arrange a small amount of each prepared vegetable and beef over the rice.
  3. Drizzle sesame oil over the ingredients. Top with an optional fried egg and serve with gochujang sauce.
  4. Mix all the ingredients together and enjoy!
